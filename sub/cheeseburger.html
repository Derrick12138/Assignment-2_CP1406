Cheeseburger


$6.00



Cut hamburger buns in half, slice tomatoes, mince onions, and wash lettuce for later use.
Add an appropriate amount of honey to the salad dressing to make the taste more rounded.
Add salt, black pepper, and Worcestershire sauce to ground beef and toss to combine.
Beat the beef balls until elastic and press into hamburger meat.
Melt butter, saute chopped onion until pale golden and set aside.
Fry the hamburger meat for 3 minutes on each side, add the cheese slices and cheese,
cover the pan until the cheese melts
