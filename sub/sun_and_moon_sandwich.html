Sun and Moon Sandwich


$6.50



Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and set aside;
Heat a pan, add a proper amount of oil, and pour in the egg mixture;
After the eggs have solidified, pour them out for later use;
Add ham slices and fry;
Line the bottom with slices of whole-wheat bread,
and stack the slices of tomatoes and cheese in order;
Add ham slices and egg slices;
Finally, cover with a slice of whole wheat bread;
