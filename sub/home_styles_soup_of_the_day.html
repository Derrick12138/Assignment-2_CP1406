Home styles soup of the day


$5.95



Peel the radish and ginger separately and cut them well
Put the spare ribs into the basin, add flour and salt and knead for a few minutes,
then rinse with water
Put the ribs, white radish, and ginger slices into the casserole in turn,
add enough water at one time, cover and cook for 30 minutes,
cook until chicken essence and salt are added to taste, stir evenly,
and then turn off the heat and serve.
